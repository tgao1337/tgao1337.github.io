I worked with the U.S. Army Research Laboratory and Skidmore, Owings & Merrill to optimize steel trusses. Using a topology optimization method in MATLAB, I created two trusses: one with a single loading case and another that supports loads from two directions.

Projects

Truss Comparison

I compared the effectiveness of the Michell truss theory and topology optimization methods of creating truss by physically testing them. 50,000 pounds of force was applied to the trusses and high definition cameras capture the movement to calculate the strain. The truss with the strain evenly distributed throughout the member was considered more efficient.
